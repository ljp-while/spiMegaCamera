Arduino UNO
==================
.. note::
    This demo is based on a 5MP camera

Hardware connection
******************************************

==========   =========================  
Camera pin   Development board pins    
==========   ========================= 
VCC          5V
GND          GND
SCK          13
MISO         12
MOSI         11
CS           7
==========   =========================
